Privacy Policy

Last updated 1 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how UAE Mortgage Tools ("we", "us") handles information when you use this website. We have designed the site to collect as little personal data as possible.

The calculators run in your browser

Every calculation on this site happens locally, in your own browser. The figures you type in — income, debts, property price and so on — are not sent to us, not stored, and not shared. Refresh the page and they are gone.

Information we may collect

  • Analytics: we may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand which pages are visited and how the site performs, using aggregated, non-identifying data.
  • If you contact us by email, we receive the email address and message you send, and use them only to reply.
  • If you submit a lead form (where offered), we collect the details you provide and, with your consent, pass them to a licensed mortgage broker. This feature is clearly labelled wherever it appears.

Cookies and advertising

We may display advertising through third-party networks such as Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices.
  • Most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings.

Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you and request that we delete it. Because we store so little, in most cases there is nothing to delete. Contact us using the details below for any privacy request.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above shows when it last changed.
